History & Culture

Lagos, a major African financial center, began as a fishing and farming settlement. Today, it is a bustling metropolis known for its vibrant music scene, rich Yoruba heritage, and as the heartbeat of Nigeria's economy.

Demographics

As the most populous city in Nigeria and Africa, Lagos is home to over 20 million people. It is a melting pot of cultures, featuring a young, tech-savvy population and a growing middle class that drives innovation and entrepreneurship.
