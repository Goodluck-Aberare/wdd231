True Light Global Resource Site Plan

Strategic Planning for Digital Presence

Site Name

True Light Global Resource

Reason: This name maintains the established brand identity of the company. It conveys "True" (reliability) and "Light" (energy/solution), which are essential attributes in the petroleum and logistics industry.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the website is to provide a digital hub that showcases the company's capabilities in oil and gas logistics, petroleum procurement (PMS, AGO, ATK, LPG), and truck maintenance. It acts as a professional bridge for B2B clients to verify credibility and initiate service requests.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A fleet manager is looking for a reliable supplier for bulk Automotive Gas Oil (AGO) and wants to confirm the company's quality assurance and delivery capacity.
  • Scenario 2: A logistics company needs urgent maintenance and spare parts for their heavy-duty trucks and is checking if True Light Global Resource offers on-site or specialized technical support.

Color Scheme

Deep Industrial Blue (#003366): Used for headers, primary navigation, and main headings to convey trust and stability.

Safety Orange (#FF8C00): Used for call-to-action buttons, accents, and borders to signify the energy/oil sector and highlight important links.

Typography

Heading Font: Segoe UI / Sans-Serif

Selected for its modern, clean look that ensures high readability across all screen sizes.

Body Font: Arial / Helvetica

A standard professional font used for paragraphs and list items to ensure consistency and fast loading times.
